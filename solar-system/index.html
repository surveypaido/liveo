    About this visualizer

    Planet positions are computed live using real Keplerian orbital elements (the same family of equations astronomers use), not a looping animation. Distances, orbital periods, and rotation are all drawn from real physical data.

    Positions are highly accurate for the present era. Dates before roughly 1000 CE or after 3000 CE are longer-range estimates — the underlying physics is real, but small uncertainties grow the further a date sits from today.

    Sizes are shown in visual scale by default (enlarged for visibility) — toggle to true scale to see just how empty space really is.
